Your Paneer Kohlapuri recipe

A typical Maharastrian taste profile,with very rich,complex spice notes,prepare a cricpy parantha or steamed rice, along with some amti dal or kachumber.

Total Time: 50-55 minutes

Ingridents

  • Cocunut : 1cup (70gms approx.) grated
  • Dry Red Chillis : 4 pieces
  • Onion : 2,finely chopped
  • Paneer:750gms;cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. In a karahi, dry grated cocunut,cloves,cinamon,whole pepper,red chillis, poppin seed

    2. Time:25 minutes

  2. Heat the oil in karahi and salute the onions till soft and translucent, add ginger-garlic paste and oil begins to seperate

    3. Time : 10 minutes

  3. Add the coarsely group spices,mix well and fry 5-1 minutes. Add water,apnerr.Cook

    4. Time : 20 minutes
